Partner with Us: Join Our Mission to Educate, Empower, and Inspire Through Music

A Shared Passion for Music. A Shared Mission for Education and Change - Empower. Inspire. Transform.

Welcome to More In Music’s Partnership Page! We are excited to invite you to join our mission to educate, inspire, and empower those interested in the music industry. At More In Music, we are dedicated to creating a culture of support and unity through innovative programming and community engagement. By partnering with us, you can play a crucial role in shaping the future of music.

Why Your Support Matters?

  • Empower Each Other:

    • At More In Music, we believe in the profound impact of shared experiences. Through our Peer-to-Peer programs, we create a nurturing environment where industry professionals and enthusiasts share their knowledge and stories. Your partnership helps amplify these stories of resilience and success, fostering a culture of unity, mutual support and encouragement.

  • Youth the Future:

    • Our Youth-to-Future programs are dedicated to connecting emerging talent with seasoned professionals. By donating or partnering on educational programs - panels, workshops, mentorships, digital programming, and more, you help bridge the gap between aspiration and achievement by helping us offer practical education and hands-on experience. Your involvement directly contributes to equipping professionals with the skills and opportunities they need to thrive in their careers.

  • Bridge the Future:

    • Our Youth-to-Future programs are dedicated to connecting emerging talent with seasoned professionals. By funding panels, workshops, mentorships, and digital programming, you help bridge the gap between aspiration and achievement by helping us offer practical education and hands-on experience. Your involvement directly contributes to equipping professionals with the skills and opportunities they need to thrive in their careers.

  • Cultivate the Community:

    • Joining us means becoming a part of a larger community dedicated to cultural enrichment and personal growth. Your support not only enhances our programs but also strengthens the community bonds that make our work possible.

  • Celebrate Together:

    • We value the heart and soul of our partners. Your contribution and partnership is more than financial support—it’s a testament to shared values and a commitment to nurturing all generations of industry professionals. We celebrate your role in our journey and ensure you’re recognized for your support.


How to Partner with Us?

  • Event Sponsorship:

    • Help us bring our events to life. From panels, workshops, and conferences your support ensures these experiences are impactful and accessible. We offer opportunities for meaningful engagement and recognition.

  • Program Sponsorship:

    • Support specific initiatives that resonate with your passion. Whether it’s a mentorship program or a digital series, your backing makes a direct difference in the professional development of industry professionals.

  • Personal/Corporate Giving & Program Partnership:

    • Contribute through program partnership and donations that align with you or your company’s values. Your support sustains our mission and extends our reach, creating more opportunities and supports in helping with career development for new and existing talent and job pipeline opportunities .

  • In-Kind Contributions:

    • Offer products or services to enhance our programs and events. Your in-kind donations are a vital part of our success and deeply valued.

  • Strategic Collaborations:

    • Explore long-term partnerships that align with your goals. Together, we can create lasting impact and continue to inspire current and future leaders in music.


Let’s Make a Difference Together

We invite you to join us in our mission to educate and empower. Your support can transform lives, bridge gaps, and inspire dreams through education. Connect with us to discuss how we can work together to make a meaningful impact.



Join us in making a difference, and let’s work together to create a future where every professional has the chance to learn and grow.

Become a partner today and help us create a symphony of change.
