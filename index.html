I'M MY WILDEST DREAM COLLECTION


Introducing the I’m My Wildest Dreams campaign. More In Music connects established industry experts in the music industry with aspiring professionals who – like all budding talent – grows, flourishes, and reaches their full potential when they have guidance from those who have already traveled similar paths.

Whether you’re currently living your younger self’s wildest dreams, or are putting in the work to make your future self proud, we hope this campaign inspires reflection on where you’ve come from, where you are, and where you’re going. For the community who’s been with us from the beginning, we’re unveiling an exclusive Wildest Dreams collection.

When you make a donation of $50 or more to More In Music today, you’ll be the first to receive the Wildest Dreams t-shirt, long-sleeve, or hoodie.

Between now and December 31st, our goal is to raise $10,000 that directly supports More In Music’s Student Cohort program as well as our education and career development initiatives.

All donations made by December 31st will receive the collection ahead of its official release in Early 2026.
